White-throated Sparrow Tarsus

We are unsure whether tarsus length is a reliable measure of body size. If tarsus length is functionally equivalent to a measurement such as wing chord, then it does not make sense to continue measuring it in the field. Here we will investigate how measurements relate to tarsus length.

Visualizing data

Well look at some plots of wing chord, sex, and mass vs tarsus as well as sex vs wing chord length. Missing values for morph pcr likely precludes looking at how morph relates to tarsus length for now.

Figure 1. Graphs of wing chord length (mm), mass (g), and sex vs tarsus length (mm) as well as sex vs wing chord length (mm).


There may be a correlation of both mass and wing chord length to tarsus length. However, this may not be statistically significant. There appears to be a greater difference between wing chord lengths than tarsus length between sexes. Looks like there are a couple of outlier points for tarsus length.
